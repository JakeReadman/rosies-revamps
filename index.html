ABOUT

Hi, I’m Rosie! A fully insured painter decorator based in Brighton & Hove. I am committed to delivering a professional, friendly service at a high standard no matter the scale. The main passion and drive in my work is in your happiness when a space has been revamped into something brighter and new.

I also have a dog named Goose, who will be making plenty of appearances in photos and stories of when I’m in your property (and I always love a brew!)
